AI-Powered WhatsApp Outreach Project

Project Objective

This project showcases a generative AI bot designed to automate the outreach and qualification process for TheFork Festival. The primary goal is to increase the efficiency of our sales team by allowing the bot to handle initial contact, answer common questions, and intelligently filter leads, freeing up Account Managers to focus on high-value conversations and relationship building.

The No-Cost Prototype Stack

We have successfully built this working prototype using a stack of robust and free-tier services, proving the concept without financial investment.

Future Roadmap

This successful prototype demonstrates the viability of this approach. The recommended next steps are:

  • 1

    Internal Pilot

    Test the bot with a small, controlled group of restaurants to gather real-world data and measure performance metrics against manual outreach.

  • 2

    Refine Knowledge Base

    Continuously add new questions, answers, and objection-handling tactics to the Google Doc as we learn from real interactions, making the bot smarter over time.

  • 3

    Explore Full CRM Integration

    Plan the migration from Google Sheets to a full Salesforce integration to automatically create tasks and manage leads directly within the CRM for maximum sales team efficiency.
